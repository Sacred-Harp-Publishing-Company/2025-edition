# SWEET CANAAN

Oh, who will come and go with me?
I am bound for the land of Ca-naan.
I’m bound fair Ca-naan’s land to see,
I am bound for the land of Ca-naan.

[chorus]
O Ca-naan, sweet Ca-naan,
I’m bound for the land of Ca-naan;
Sweet Ca-naan, ’tis my hap-py home,
I am bound for the land of Ca-naan.

I’ll join with those who’re gone be-fore,
I am bound for the land of Ca-naan.
Where sin and sor-row are no more,
I am bound for the land of Ca-naan.

------

Oh, who will come and go with me?
I am bound for the land of Canaan.
I’m bound fair Canaan’s land to see,
I am bound for the land of Canaan.

[chorus]
O Canaan, sweet Canaan,
I’m bound for the land of Canaan;
Sweet Canaan, ’tis my happy home,
I am bound for the land of Canaan.

I’ll join with those who’re gone before,
I am bound for the land of Canaan.
Where sin and sorrow are no more,
I am bound for the land of Canaan.
