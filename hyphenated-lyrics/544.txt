# YOUTHFUL BLESSINGS

May God His rich-est bless-ings give
To you, my dear young friend,
And make you use-ful while you live,
And save you at the end.

Your youth-ful days will soon be gone,
For time is fly-ing by;
Our days and years pass swift-ly on,
We’ll soon be called to die.
