# CHEVES

Grace, ’tis a most de-light-ful theme,
’Tis grace that res-cues guilt-y man,
’Tis grace di-vine, all-con-qu’ring, free,
Or it had nev-er res-cued me.

------

Grace, ’tis a most delightful theme,
’Tis grace that rescues guilty man,
’Tis grace divine, all-conqu’ring, free,
Or it had never rescued me.
