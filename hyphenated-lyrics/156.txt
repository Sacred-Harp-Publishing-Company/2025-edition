# JESUS ROSE

Go and tell His dis-ci-ples,
He has ris-en from the dead.
Je-sus rose, breth-ren,
He has ris-en from the dead,
Through the earth and through the sky.

------

Go and tell His disciples,
He has risen from the dead.
Jesus rose, brethren,
He has risen from the dead,
Through the earth and through the sky.
